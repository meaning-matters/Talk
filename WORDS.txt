This file contains the preferred words and terms used in texts 
shown to the user.  This is important for those withing the
initial texts in English, as well as for translators.  Translators
must be instruction keep words and terms consistent in the target
languages.

The format: Preferred words are in CAPITALS, rejected alternatives
are in small letters and inside ().

* The time a number lasts: DURATION (period)

* PHONE NUMBER (number, telephone number)

* ISSUE, PROBLEM, ... (error)
